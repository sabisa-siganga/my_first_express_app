About Us

TEH, or The Eatery Hub, is a globally one of the prominently recognized fast food brand specializing in chicken, burgers, variety of desserts and drinks. It is the second-largest restaurant chain worldwide, Hearty Meals restaurant. With over 19 locations in more than 11 countries, The EateryHub is known for its Original Recipe pressure- grilled chicken and burgers and has expanded its menu to include fried chicken, chicken strips, sandwiches, wings, drinks and desserts. Founded by Mr and Mrs S & S in 2017, TEH operates as a subsidiary of Yummy Meals! Brands, which also owns House of Pizza and Chicken Bell. The brand has a strong presence in social media and marketing, with millions of people exposed to TEH commercials each week. Franchisees receive comprehensive support through Yummy Meals! Value Network, including brand recognition, customer attraction, training, financing options, and more. TEh serves over 12 thousand customers daily, primarily through dine in, take-out and delivery services.
